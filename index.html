About Us

Learn CS offers different packages for your different learning needs. With practicality in mind, we structure the modules to teach core skills while reinforcing previous lessons and topic.

Each module is project based, so you learn by doing, not by blindly listening. For each meeting, we build upon the previous topics and introduce just enough to keep things fresh and not overwhelm you.

Learn CS is ran and maintained by John Alcher, a Multimedia Technology graduate hailing from Malolos. He started self-teaching programming after graduation, and grew a special love for the Python programming language.
He's currently taking his bachelors in Computer Science with a major in software development.
